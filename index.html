About The Stew

Hey there, I'm Josh, a Programmer and Designer from California. I've been feeling the rush of making games since I was 16, and am currently an Indie developer. I recently graduated from CSU Channel Islands, studying Computer Science with two minors in Game Design & Math. Around these parts you'll see some fun projects I've worked on; from personal passion projects to school-turned game design projects. I love the challenge of programming, the awe of pouring myself into a creative endeavor and witnessing the outcomes, and hopefully my work fervently screams that desire. Sometimes I also dabble in the art of website-ing, so enjoy the results :)
